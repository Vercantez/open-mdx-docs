---
title: Callouts
description: Note, Tip, Warning, Info, and Check callouts for highlighting important information.
---

Use callouts to pull important information out of the flow of the page.

## Variants

<Note>
  Notes share neutral context that is useful but not urgent.
</Note>

<Tip title="Pro tip">
  Tips highlight recommended patterns and shortcuts.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Warnings call out pitfalls that can break a deploy or surprise readers.
</Warning>

<Info>
  Info callouts surface supporting detail without stopping the reader.
</Info>

<Check>
  Checks confirm that a step completed successfully.
</Check>

## Usage

```mdx
<Note>
  Notes share neutral context.
</Note>

<Tip title="Pro tip">
  Tips highlight recommended patterns.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Warnings call out pitfalls.
</Warning>
```
